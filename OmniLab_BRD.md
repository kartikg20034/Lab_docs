# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## Project: OmniLab Virtual Science Framework (Class 9 & 10)

### 1. Executive Summary
OmniLab is an AI-powered, cloud-synced virtual laboratory designed for CBSE Class 9 and 10 students. The platform aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application by providing high-fidelity simulations that allow for safe experimentation, mistake-driven learning, and formal AI-guided tutoring.

### 2. Business Objectives
* **Practical Excellence:** Provide a digital environment for over 80+ CBSE-aligned science activities.
* **Mistake-Driven Learning:** Shift from "correct-only" paths to allowing students to witness the consequences of "dangerous" mistakes in a safe virtual space.
* **Scalability:** Create a framework that supports rapid content expansion via simple configuration updates.

### 3. Target Audience
* **Primary:** Class 9 & 10 students for self-study and practical exam preparation.
* **Secondary:** Educators looking for interactive demonstration tools.

### 4. Key Success Metrics
* **Autonomous Completion:** Student completes a lab successfully without triggering non-safety AI hints.
* **Learning Efficacy:** Reduction in "Critical Mistakes" over repeated sessions.

### 5. Scope & Constraints
* **In-Scope:** All Chemistry/Physics chapters for Grade 9 & 10, Cloud progress sync, Local event logging, Formal AI tutoring.
* **Out-of-Scope:** Live teacher-student classroom sessions, gamified leaderboards.
* **Constraints:** Requires active internet for AI and cloud persistence; optimized for mid-range mobile hardware.
